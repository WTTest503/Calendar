Upcoming Events

  • Put Fun First

    Competition is a part of any game. But win or lose, we should strive to keep it friendly and respectful. Provide other players with a positive experience and everyone will have fun.

  • Everyone is Welcome

    Our community includes a range of diverse people and we are dedicated to bringing people together through their shared love of games. No matter who sits down at the table with us, we make them feel safe and welcome.

  • Keep Your Area Clean

    Each of us needs to help maintain a neat and clean community area. Please use the provided trash cans.

  • Be a Teacher

    Helping a new player to learn the rules is the most valuable thing any person can provide to our community. Discussing strategies, offering tips, or constructively critiquing game play decisions after games are concluded helps both participants to become better players.

  • Follow the Rules

    All players must comply with all local laws and event rules. Be fair and honest. Foul or abusive language will not be tolerated.

  • Trade Etiquette

    Trades are allowed but solicitation and/or purchasing is not allowed. This includes any monetary transactions concluded either on the property (including the parking lot) or electronically.
